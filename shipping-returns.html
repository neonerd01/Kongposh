Shipping

Orders are made to order and typically ship within 5–10 business days of order confirmation. You'll receive tracking details via WhatsApp once your order is on its way.

Domestic shipping usually takes 3–7 business days after dispatch. International shipping times vary by destination — message us to confirm before ordering.

Shipping costs are calculated based on your location and will be shared before you confirm payment.

Returns & Exchanges

Because every piece is handmade to order, we're unable to accept returns for change of mind. If there's a problem with your order — damage in transit, a fault, or a mismatch with what was agreed — contact us within 3 days of delivery.

We'll work with you on a repair, remake, or refund depending on the situation.

Custom orders require approval of a photo before final shipping, so please review carefully at that stage — this is your chance to request changes before the piece is packed.

Questions about a specific order? Contact us and we'll help sort it out.
