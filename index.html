Our story

Two crafts that were never meant to meet.

Loop & Lustre started on a kitchen table with a ball of cotton yarn and a secondhand soldering torch. We still work that way — small batches, one bench, no assembly line. If a piece takes an extra evening to get right, it takes an extra evening.

Every ring is filed by hand. Every stitch is counted. Nothing leaves the bench until it's something we'd wear ourselves.
