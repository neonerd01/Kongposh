Our Story

Handmade, made for you

KONGPOSH started as a small handmade-crafts practice and grew into a home for embroidery, crochet, resin jewellery, and more — every single piece made to order, by hand, for the person who asked for it.

We don’t keep shelves of finished stock. Instead, every order starts a conversation — about colors, sizes, and the little details that make a piece feel personal — before we pick up a needle, hook, or resin mold.
